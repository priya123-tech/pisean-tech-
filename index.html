OUR FEATURES

Explore the innovative features of our RFID-powered self-checkout system, designed to enhance efficiency and security in retail operations.

Product Scanning

Our system uses advanced RFID technology to scan products instantly. Simply walk through the RFID gateway with your items, and our system will automatically identify each product, ensuring accuracy and speed.

With RFID, multiple items can be scanned simultaneously, eliminating the need for manual barcode scanning. This significantly reduces checkout time and enhances the overall customer experience.

Seamless Checkout

Our self-checkout system is designed for ease of use. Customers can easily review their items on the screen, make adjustments, and proceed to payment without the need for assistance.

The intuitive interface guides users through each step of the process, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free checkout experience.

Q: How does the RFID self-checkout system work?

Our RFID-powered self-checkout system utilizes an RFID cart and implements a double verification system at the payment gateway to prevent theft. Customers scan items using the AI cart, and at the gateway, there's an additional verification step to ensure that all items have been properly scanned and paid for, enhancing security and reducing the risk of theft.
